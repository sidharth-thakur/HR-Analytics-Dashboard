# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Tableau Public** to analyze employee attrition patterns.
The dashboard transforms raw HR data into meaningful insights to help organizations understand **why employees leave** and how to improve retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze **employee attrition trends**
* Identify **high-risk departments and groups**
* Study the impact of **age, gender, education, and job satisfaction**
* Enable **interactive filtering** for deeper insights
* Support **data-driven HR decision-making**

---

## 📁 Dataset Information

* Total Employees: **1,470**
* Attrition Count: **237**
* Attrition Rate: **16.12%**
* Average Age: **37**

### Key Features:

* Employee Demographics (Age, Gender)
* Department & Job Role
* Education Field & Level
* Job Satisfaction Rating
* Attrition Status (Yes/No)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate
* Active Employees
* Average Age

### 🔹 Visualizations

* 📌 Pie Chart → Department-wise Attrition
* 📌 Bar Chart → Employee Distribution by Age Group
* 📌 Matrix Table → Job Satisfaction by Job Role
* 📌 Horizontal Bar Chart → Education Field-wise Attrition
* 📌 Donut Charts → Gender-wise Attrition across Age Groups

### 🔹 Interactive Feature

* 🎛️ **Education Level Filter**
  Allows users to dynamically explore data across different education categories.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 🔺 Highest attrition in **Sales Department (56%)**
* 👶 Employees **under 25** have the highest attrition
* 👨 Male attrition is higher than female
* 🎓 **Life Sciences & Medical fields** show high attrition
* 😞 Low job satisfaction strongly linked to employee exit
* 👥 Workforce concentrated in **30–38 age group**

---

## ⚠️ Problem Areas Identified

* Sales Department (high turnover)
* Young employees (early career instability)
* Low satisfaction roles (Sales Executive, Lab Technician)
* Life Sciences background employees

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Improve **career growth & compensation** in Sales
* Introduce **mentorship programs** for young employees
* Enhance **job satisfaction initiatives**
* Develop **targeted retention strategies**
